Featured Video of Footnote of Youth

Explanation

This video shows Dodong's journey in "Footnote of Youth," focusing on his early marriage and the challenges that come with it. It highlights how Dodong, in his youthful idealism, rushes into marriage and then faces the unexpected burdens of responsibility. The video shows the tough realities of growing up too fast and dealing with the heavy responsibilities that come with commitment. It also emphasizes how such decisions early on do not only affect the person who made them but also his family and future.
