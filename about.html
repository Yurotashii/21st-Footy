We're the Group 5!

We are Group 5 from Grade 12 Integrity, a team of passionate individuals committed to creating meaningful and impactful projects. Our current focus is on crafting a website dedicated to Footnote to Youth, the timeless story by José García Villa. Through this website, we aim to dive deep into the themes, lessons, and relevance of the story in today's world. We hope to provide readers with fresh insights and an engaging platform to explore the narrative's core message about life, youth, and responsibility. Join us as we celebrate this literary masterpiece and make its message resonate with modern audiences.
